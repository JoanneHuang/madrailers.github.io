About MadRailers

Welcome! This page is the nexus of information about the MadRailers Meetup; a Madison, WI-based technology group focused on Ruby and Ruby-on-Rails.

You might be thinking "What's up with this page?" Well- we're looking to crowdsource the design and implementation of the site. We want to keep it as a single page that includes links to our social media and content videos, but within those general guidelines please fork the repo and start submitting pull requests!
